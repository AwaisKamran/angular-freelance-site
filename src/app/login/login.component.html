Our Freelancers
Our Mission
Our mission is to help individuals and families make a house their home, regardless of where they are. Our mission includes providing freedom and giving back autonomy to freelancers so they may regain control of their personal and professional lives.
Our Vision
Our vision is to ease the process or finding and recruiting freelancers depending on the needs and means of various households. We also aim to revolutionise the job market in the hope that more people will become freelancers and self-employed.
Our Values
Our values are fairness, care, generosity, freedom, diversity and community. Our values form the foundation of our professional practice and we encourage interaction and participation from our diverse communities.
